Skill Check

Learning Mobile App

About

Skill Check is an innovative and brand new mobile application that was made for learning and teaching purposes. People tend to learn and gain new information and skills, and suddenly, a lot of learning methods appeared, due to WEB expansion. We offer you a unique learning method that is connected with a simple swipe function.

Our Process

We are creating a tinder-like mobile app, where people can share their skills, and choose others. You can learn new skills, but bear in mind that at the same time, you will teach someone else. Formula is simple: I teach you, You teach me.

Our Approach

We want to create a swiping function app, where you can find a person that will teach you certain skills. When matching is being done between two persons, they are ready to communicate and develop their skills.

Our Goal

Our goal is to make sure, that our learning method will be the most efficient. We aspire to make a platform where people can share their skills that they've been collecting for months and years, so they can spread it around the world.

Our Mission

We are aspiring to create a strong community that will share all they have collected for the long period of time, and transfere it to the other person.
